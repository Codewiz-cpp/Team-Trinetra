Mission & Innovation

Team Trinetra & Mayoor: Engineering a Lifeline

What started as a focused team of two has evolved into a powerhouse of seven members and five mentors driven by a singular mission: saving lives. At the heart of our operations is Mayoor, our custom-built uncrewed aerial system engineered for high-stakes environments. Mayoor is designed to deliver critical autonomous relief drops such as essential water bottles, emergency beacons” providing immediate, precise aid to human survivors in post-disaster scenarios.

SUAS 2026: Technology in Action for Recovery and Relief

This season, Team Trinetra is tackling the SUAS 2026 "Storm Response" challenge, exploring the vital role robotics play in community resilience and disaster recovery. Our systems are built to turn chaos into coordinated action across three critical phases: conducting rapid damage assessment to map environmental hazards, searching debris fields to deploy supplies, and collecting public safety data to support first responders. We aren't just restoring what was lost; we are leveraging technology with purpose to rebuild smarter.

MITS    TEAM TRINETRA

Representing the Madhav Institute of Technology & Science under the banner of the Dronex Aerotech club, Team Trinetra UAV is a multidisciplinary student engineering team preparing for the international SUAS 2026 competition. The team's mission is to leverage autonomous systems to advance disaster recovery frameworks, specifically focusing on aerial area inspection, hazard monitoring, and critical rescue logistics.
